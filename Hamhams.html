Hamtaro has met so many lovely hamsters during his adventures, there is no way we could introduce them all here! So here are just a few of the lovely friends you might meet in the world of Hamtaro.


Hamtaro
 His courage and adventurous spirit make him the inspirational hero of all the other hamsters. He's a lot of fun, too!

Bijou
 From France, the elegant Bijou loves her friends, ribbons and jewels. With her delicate nature, she is the Ham-Ham sweetheart.

Boss
Gruff on the outside, but sweet on the inside. Like his name suggests, this bachelor field hamster wants to be in charge

Pashmina
Responsible and dependable, Pashmina loves her precious Penelope, and her favorite pink scarf she always wears.

Penelope
The youngest hamster, Penelope is always wearing her favourite blanket over her head. The only word she can say is "Ookwee!"

Snoozer Always sleeping, but occasionally wakes up to provide practical advice and wisdom to the Hamsters when needed.
